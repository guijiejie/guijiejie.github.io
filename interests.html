<h1 style="margin-top: 0em">Research Interests</h1><br>
<p>
I have a wide range of research interests, mainly including machine learning (ML), pattern recognition (PR), data mining (DM), artificial intelligence (AI), image processing (IP), and computer vision (CV), among which ML and PR are my core research areas. Currently I am interested in the following ML/PR topics:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Adversarial machine learning</p></li>
<li><p>Self-supervised learning</p></li>
<li><p>Hashing</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
My favorite applications include:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Biometric recognition (face recognition and palmprint recognition)</p></li>
<li><p>Digit recognition</p></li>
<li><p>Text classification</p></li>
<li><p>Image classification</p></li>
<li><p>Image retrieval</p></li>
</ul>
<p>
My previous research interests or the research areas that I am very familiar with:
</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Dimensionality reduction, subspace learning, manifold learning, feature selection, metric learning, and non-negative matrix factorization </p></li>
<li><p>Sparse learning and low-rank</p></li>
<li><p>Kernel learning</p></li>
<li><p>Semi-supervised learning</p></li>
<li><p>Multi-view learning</p></li>
<li><p>Classification and clustering</p></li>
</ul>
